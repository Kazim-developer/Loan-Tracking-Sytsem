## The Problem With Excel

Excel is not the problem. Using it to manage
an active loan portfolio is.

Thousands of small lending businesses trust
their entire operation to spreadsheets — and
lose money every month because of it.

## The Hidden Cost of Errors

A single wrong formula in your loan spreadsheet
does not just affect one cell. It cascades —
wrong balance, wrong interest, wrong repayment
amount — sometimes for the entire loan term
before anyone notices.

## No Alerts Means Missed Payments

Excel is passive. It sits there and waits for
you to check it. It will never tell you that
a borrower missed their payment three weeks ago.

Without automated alerts, overdue accounts only
get noticed when you manually go looking.

## Scaling is Impossible on Spreadsheets

Managing 10 loans on Excel is inconvenient.
Managing 100 is dangerous. Managing 500 is
practically impossible.

As your lending portfolio grows, your spreadsheet
becomes slower, more complex, and more fragile.

## The Alternative

Dedicated loan tracking software like Loqvio
automates everything Excel makes you do manually
— calculations, balance updates, overdue flagging,
repayment schedules — so you can focus on growing
your lending business instead of managing a spreadsheet.
